backstage
with charlotte

Why did you start your own makeup line?

I wanted to create makeup that was easy to use and easy to choose. Much like fashion, all the colors in my range are curated into a wardrobe of 10 looks. Each includes everything necessary to transform you into a Golden Goddess, a Bombshell or whatever you’re feeling that day.

What is your


desert island


beauty product?

That’s like trying to ask a mother to choose her favorite child! I never start makeup or leave home without my Magic Cream. It's the magic dew of youth and luminosity.

Why are you and Bloomingdale's


a great match?

To me, Bloomingdale's is so iconic. I love how they're always looking for ways to set their brand apart and deliver new experiences to their customers. This is the same approach I take with my line. I simply can't wait to bring my makeup revolution to Bloomingdale's!

What is your makeup philosophy?

Give a woman the right makeup and she can
conquer the world.
