Behind the Scenes: Artist Greg Lamarche

Play On Words

100% Bloomingdale’s is all about collaboration, so partnering with the dynamic artist and full-on text messenger Greg Lamarche on our fall campaign was a brushstroke of genius, if we do say so ourselves. When we first saw his playful compositions of letters, we knew we spoke the same language—the way Greg fuses graffiti and graphics is just like our approach to fashion and fun. Greg’s work provided the perfect backdrop and a splashy design element for our campaign. This fall, we literally couldn’t spell 100% Bloomingdale’s without him.
