Michael Aram

Like an alchemy of the imagination, the Torched Collection from Michael Aram explores the transformation of metal when exposed to intense heat. Each piece is literally touched by fire, creating a one-of-a-kind look and feel, with distinctive gradations from dark charcoal gray to a subtle, smoky red. According to Aram, “This collection evokes a sense of creation and destruction and nature’s sublime yet threatening beauty.”
