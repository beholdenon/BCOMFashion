Red Lip Tips

First Step: PREP

Gently exfoliate your lips with a warm washcloth or soft toothbrush to create a smooth canvas. Then use a hydrating lip balm and wait a few minutes for it to soak in before applying your lipstick.

SHOP FOUNDATIONS & CONCEALERS

Choose the right shade

For fair complexions, look for reds with cool blue undertones. Those with medium and olive skin will look best in brown-based shades. For darker skin tones, try bright orange– and violet–based reds.

SHOP RED LIPSTICKS

Make it Last

Start by completely filling in your lips with a long-wear liner. Next, apply your first coat of lipstick, setting the color by placing a tissue over your lips and dusting a fine layer of translucent powder on top of the tissue. Complete the look by adding a second coat of lipstick.
