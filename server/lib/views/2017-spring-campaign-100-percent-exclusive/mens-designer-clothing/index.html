THE EXTRA COOL COLLAB

Pharrell Williams for G-STAR RAW

For his first collection as part owner of and full-time visionary for G-STAR RAW, the stylish producer and songwriter created 25 different versions of the brand’s iconic Elwood jeans in honor of the style’s 25th birthday. Since 12 patterns are also exclusive to us, Pharrell agreed to sit down with our Fashion Office to discuss the details of the Elwood x25.

Shop the collection

You’re famous for being a game-changing musician and producer. How do you apply that creative approach to clothing?

PW: I see my role at G-STAR RAW as Head of Imagination as another form of expression. We want to inspire people to rethink what denim can do.

What appeals to you about working with denim?

PW: I’ve been given the opportunity to explore an uncharted territory with jeans. Jeans are one of the most democratic clothing items—everyone has a pair in their closet. But what’s beautiful about them is how, as you wear them, they break in and shape into something that’s unique and original to you.

How did you approach updating the Elwood, one of G-STAR’s most famous styles?

PW: The idea of “democracy in your wardrobe” inspired our designs for the Elwood x25. We updated G-STAR’s most iconic jean in 25 unique prints, each based on different checks, camouflages and traditional patterns from all over the world. When you put these 25 prints together, you’re effectively taking them out of their historical contexts. That allows you to decide for yourself which one to wear and how to wear it. The prints really symbolize my world and the world of G-STAR coming together.

The collection includes some pretty bold patterns. How would you recommend wearing them?

PW: What’s most important right now is individuality. We made 25 different prints to make sure there was something for everyone. I don’t worry about [a pattern or color] being too much. It’s all about self-expression.
