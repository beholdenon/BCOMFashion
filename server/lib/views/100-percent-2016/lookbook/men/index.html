Canali

In Italy, men dress fearlessly in natural, bold colors like brown, burnt orange and gray–and look exceptionally good doing so! Our Fashion Team flew to Milan to score this spread of suiting and separates in a palette that looks especially sharp in the deft cuts orchestrated by Canali's creative consultant, Andrea Pompilio. The best thing about these unique jacket and trouser combinations? They all mix and match effortlessly.

“Most guys have amassed piles of solid-colored shirts, so we wanted to shake things up a bit,” says our Fashion Director Anya Deweerdt, about this capsule collection created with the tech-forward team at Theory.
