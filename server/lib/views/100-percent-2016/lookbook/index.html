Karen Millen

When we first visited Karen Millen's London atelier, we were happily surprised to see that one of her designers was hand-crafting original prints in the back. This artisanal style epitomizes a Karen Millen collection; they always incorporate the best materials and keep her design standards incredibly high. Such is the case with every piece in this exclusive capsule collection. From a little white dress to a lace-up jean and top, each piece is created with the impeccable care and style she is renowned for.
