THE LOOK:
Top cat-eye meets bottom glitter.



PERFECT FOR:
A black-tie fête and dancing ’til 2AM.



WHY WE LOVE IT:
This glam twist on winged liner adds a festive touch to a classic makeup look.

GET THE LOOK:
4

Start by rimming your lower lash line with  Tom Ford’s Eye Defining Pencil , blending it softly, to define the eye and create contrast for the glitter.

Next, create a cat-eye line with liquid liner, extending it out toward the end of your brow.

Complete the look by
layering on  Lime Crime’s
Diamond Dew  underneath
your lower lashes.

An easy,
no-mess
way to apply
glitter.

THE LOOK:
Lush lashes with a sparkly twist.


PERFECT FOR:
A late-night dinner with friends followed by barhopping.


WHY WE LOVE IT:
This sophisticated way to wear glitter requires minimal effort and looks insanely good with a velvet mini

GET THE LOOK:
4

Fill in between your eyelashes with deep brown or black eyeliner for a fuller look. Then, curl your lashes and use M·A·C Bold & Bad Lash’s tiny brush to coat your bottom lashes, followed by the bigger brush for the top.

Two different
formulas—and
brushes!

Shake well and add Estée Lauder Crystal Lash Top Coat to your top lashes only, layering on extra for added intensity.

Finish the look with matte taupe eyeshadow to let your eyelashes pop.

THE LOOK:
Perfectly defined pop lips.


PERFECT FOR:
An under-the-stars hotel party somewhere exotic.


WHY WE LOVE IT:
A bright lip is an easy statement that requires very little additional makeup.

GET THE LOOK:
4

Prep the lips by using an exfoliating lip balm. Make sure to wipe away any excess balm before applying Dior Rouge Dior Liquid Lip Stain in Impulsive Matte.

Smudge it with your finger to give it a softer effect, and don’t forget to connect the inner corners of the lips with the color.

Part concealer,
part highlighter
and totally
versatile.

To make your lips really pop, outline them with YSL Beauty Dazzling Lights Touche Éclat Pen, patting gently to blur any lines.

THE LOOK:
Just-enough-flush cheeks and an allover glow.


PERFECT FOR:
A low-key night at home with champagne and a killer cheese plate.


WHY WE LOVE IT:
Dewy skin with a little highlighter and blush is the ultimate night-in look.

GET THE LOOK:
4

Start by plumping the skin with a hydrating serum, and then apply Bobbi Brown’s Extra Illuminating Moisture Balm, which works as both a moisturizer and base.

Use Kevyn Aucoin’s Neo-Trio Palette for a soft pink on the apples of the cheeks.

Apply the bronzer on your eyelids and highlighter on your cheekbones. Finish the look with sheer gloss.

highlighter and
